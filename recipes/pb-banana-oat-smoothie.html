High Protein PB Banana Oat Smoothie

Description

This banana oat smoothie is smooth and creamy, the perfect consistency, just like a smoothie should be! You will taste hints of honey, vanilla, peanut butter, and banana. The smoothie is very filling and great for an on-the-go breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, broken in half
  • 1 cup ice
  • ¼ cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • ½ cup vanilla yogurt
  • ½ cup skim milk
  • 1 ½ tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon honey, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste

Steps

  1. Put banana, ice, oats, yogurt, skim milk, peanut butter, honey, and cinnamon, respectively, in a blender; blend until smooth.
